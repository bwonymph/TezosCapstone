TezosDeveloper Curriculum

The Tezos Capstone Developer Curriculum is a set of challenges that will provide the knowledge and skills needed to become a Tezos developer. These challenges will cover foundational Tezos concepts, introducing key tools, best practices, and other aspects of smart contract and dApp development including building a front end.

  • Part 1: "Learn the Basics"
  • Part 2: “Practice Challenge”
  • Part 3: “Real World Challenge”
  • Part 4: "Hackathon"

Components

Part 1: "Learn the Basics"

Cover foundational Tezos concepts, introducing key tools, best practices, and other aspects of smart contract and dApp development including building a front end.

Part 2: “Practice Challenge”

Continue your Tezos developer journey by learning how to program with Liquidity to program smart contracts and connecting them to your front-end.

Part 3: “Real World Challenge”

Test your skills by coming up with your own projects and smart contracts based on real world scenarios.
